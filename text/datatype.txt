Data from the Fisheries Catch monitoring program includes information about fishers and vessels at Turneffe Atoll Marine Reserve (TAMR), as well as the catch size and key measurements for Queen Conch, Caribbean Spiny Lobster, and finfish. 
Data from the Long-term Atoll Monitoring Program (LAMP) includes either (1) only Queen Conch, or (2) Queen Conch, Caribbean Spiny Lobster, and finfish abundance and key measurements values at Turneffe Atoll Marine Reserve (TAMR). Queen Conch LAMP data at TAMR stretches back to 2013 onwards, whereas general LAMP data stretches back to 2023 onwards. Data for this program are collected annually where possible. 
Spawning aggregation (SPAG) data includes information collected on fish spawning aggregations (FSAs) at Turneffe Atoll Marine Reserve (TAMR), including species observed, counts, and environmental conditions during dives. Two primary methodologies for SPAG include visual census and laser. SPAG data at TAMR is available from 2022 onwards.
